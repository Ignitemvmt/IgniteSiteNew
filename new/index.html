Who We Are

A community of burning ones

We're a Gen-Z movement of disciples ready to give it all for the One who gave His all.

Ignite Belize is a 501(c)(3) faith-based nonprofit based in Spanish Lookout. We exist to see Belize transformed by the gospel — one young heart at a time — through worship, leadership development, and bold evangelism.

This isn't just an organization. It's a holy fire spreading through a generation.
