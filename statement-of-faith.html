// What we believe

On the record

Seven convictions that frame our doctrine — the bedrock under every Burn Night, every Launch session, every conversation.

// 01

The Scriptures

We believe the Bible — both Old and New Testaments — is the inspired, inerrant, and authoritative Word of God, and the final rule for faith and practice.

// 02

God

We believe in one God, eternally existing in three Persons — Father, Son, and Holy Spirit — perfect in holiness, infinite in wisdom, and unchanging in love.

// 03

Jesus Christ

We believe in the deity of Jesus Christ, His virgin birth, sinless life, atoning death on the cross, bodily resurrection, ascension, and personal return in power and glory.

// 04

The Holy Spirit

We believe in the present ministry of the Holy Spirit, by whose indwelling the believer is empowered to live a godly life and is gifted for service in the Church.

// 05

Salvation

We believe salvation is by grace alone, through faith alone, in Christ alone — apart from works — and that all who repent and believe in Jesus are born again into eternal life.

// 06

The Church

We believe the Church is the Body of Christ — a global family of believers commissioned to make disciples, love one another, and proclaim the Gospel to every nation.

// 07

The Mission

We believe every follower of Jesus is sent — that the Great Commission is the mandate of the whole Church, and that this generation in Belize is called to carry the Gospel with fresh fire.

"Your kingdom come, your will be done, on earth as it is in heaven."

Matthew 6:10
